### Decision Criteria

**PROCEED to Phase 1 if:**
- ✅ >50% completion rate on any variant
- ✅ >60% scroll to "How It Works"
- ✅ >2 min average time on page
- ✅ Positive qualitative feedback

**ITERATE if:**
- ⚠️ 30-50% completion (messaging needs work)
- ⚠️ High drop-off at specific question
- ⚠️ Wrong demographics completing

**PIVOT/KILL if:**
- ❌ <30% completion across all variants
- ❌ Cannot recruit target audience
- ❌ Results feel generic/obvious
- ❌ Zero sharing behavior
